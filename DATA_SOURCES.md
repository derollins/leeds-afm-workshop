# Data Sources

The sample AFM images and videos in `data/samples/` are drawn from the following
publicly available datasets. Please cite the original sources if you reuse this data
beyond the workshop.

---

Camargo Jr., S., Rocha, G. M., Ferreira, F. V., & Paciornik, S. (2019).
*AFM images and processing routine* [Dataset]. Mendeley Data.
https://doi.org/10.17632/6dzmrjngcg.3

Chau, C. C., Gupta, V., Heath, G. R., Wälti, C., & Actis, P. (2026).
*Data for visualizing and quantifying microRNA-induced DNA origami separation at the nanoscale* [Dataset].
University of Leeds. https://doi.org/10.5518/1770

Gamill, M., Holmes, E., Catley, T., Wiggins, L., Pyne, A., Whittle, S.,
Shephard, N., Bryant, H., Provan, J., Colloms, S. D., Rusková, R., Main, K.,
Gilhooly, N., Gambus, A., & Račko, D. (2025).
*DNA knots, catenanes, and replication intermediates via high-resolution AFM* [Dataset].
ORDA, University of Sheffield. https://doi.org/10.15131/shef.data.27143238.v1

Heath, G. R. (2026).
*Annexin V assembly dynamics on lipid bilayers by High-Speed AFM* (Version 1) [Dataset].
Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19254504

Irrem-Laareb Mohammad, Fernández, A., Taulés, M., Fourgous, E., Boulbik, Y.,
Le Roux, A.-L., Roche, S., Pons, M., Giannotti, M., & Sirvent, A. (2025).
*Lipid-driven SRC self-association modulates its transformation capacity* [Dataset].
Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14943298

Jelena Vukajlovic-Plestina (2019).
*Fundamental aspects to localize self-catalyzed III-V nanowires on silicon* [Dataset].
Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.2541732

Meinhardt, A., Qi, P., David, C., Maximov, I., & Keller, T. F. (2024).
*Raw data for manuscript A. Meinhardt et al., “A pathway towards sub-10 nm surface
nanostructures utilizing block copolymer crystallization control”* [Dataset].
Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14202742

Pyne, A., Noy, A., Main, K., Velasco Berrelleza, V., Piperakis, M., Mitchenall, L.,
Cugliandolo, F., Beton, J., Stevenson, C., Hoogenboom, B., Bates, A., Maxwell, A.,
& Harris, S. (2021).
*Atomic force microscopy and atomistic molecular dynamics simulation data to resolve
structures of negatively-supercoiled DNA minicircles at base-pair resolution* [Dataset].
Figshare. https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.13116890.v1

Simmel, F. C. (2020).
*AFM raw data – Aghebat Rafat et al., 2020* [Dataset].
Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.3685805
