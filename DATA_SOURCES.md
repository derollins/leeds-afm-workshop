# Data Sources

The sample AFM images and videos in `data/samples/` are drawn from the following
publicly available datasets. Please cite the original sources if you reuse this data
beyond the workshop.

---

**Meinhardt, A., Qi, P., David, C., Maximov, I., & Keller, T. F. (2024).**
Raw data for manuscript A. Meinhardt et al., A pathway towards sub-10 nm surface
nanostructures utilizing block copolymer crystallization control.
Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14202742

**Irrem-Laareb Mohammad, Fernández, A., Taulés, M., Fourgous, E., Boulbik, Y., Le Roux, A.-L., Roche, S., Pons, M., Giannotti, M., & Sirvent, A. (2025).**
Lipid-driven SRC self-association modulates its transformation capacity [Data set].
Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14943298

**Simmel, F. C. (2020).**
AFM raw data - Aghebat Rafat et al., 2020 [Data set].
Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.3685805

**Pyne, A., Noy, A., Main, K., Velasco Berrelleza, V., Piperakis, M., A. Mitchenall, L., Cugliandolo, F., Beton, J., Stevenson, C., Hoogenboom, B., Bates, A., Maxwell, A., & Harris, S. (2021).**
Atomic force microscopy and atomistic molecular dynamics simulation data to resolve
structures of negatively-supercoiled DNA minicircles at base-pair resolution
[Data set]. Figshare. https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.13116890.v1

**Heath, G. R. (2025).**
AFM BioMed 2025 - AFM Data - Bacteriorhodopsin Membrane Imaging [Data set].
Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17105214

**Chau, C. C., Gupta, V., Heath, G. R., Wälti, C., & Actis, P. (2026).**
Data for Visualizing and Quantifying microRNA-Induced DNA Origami Separation at the
Nanoscale. University of Leeds [Dataset]. https://doi.org/10.5518/1770
